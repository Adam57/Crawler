Thousands of people packed into a rain-swept Trafalgar Square in Central London yesterday to celebrate Chinese New Year.

To mark the year of the snake, performers from China and Britain had paraded through the streets of Chinatown before a traditional dragon eyes-dotting ceremony on the stage at Trafalgar Square, and a flying lion dance in front of the facade of the National Gallery.
