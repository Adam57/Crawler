Katy Perry, John Mayer Look In Love At Pre-Grammys Party (PHOTOS)

Posted: 02/10/2013 2:14 pm EST

Katy Perry and John Mayer cozied up at Clive Davis' pre-Grammy party on Feb. 9.

Katy Perry may be ditching John Mayer in favor of bringing Allison Williams as her date to tonight's Grammy Awards, but the couple were definitely together and looking quite in love at Clive Davis' Pre-Grammy Gala and Salute to Industry Icons in Beverly Hills on Feb. 9.

We have to admit, we never expected this pair to last when they first started dating last summer, especially since Katy Perry only finalized her divorce from Russell Brand months earlier, and John Mayer is well, John Mayer. But the couple, who wore matching white outfit's at the pre-Grammy bash, are still going strong.

A partygoer told Us Weekly that Perry, 28, and Mayer, 35, "seemed really happy and content," which appropriately echoes Mayer's remarks in a recent interview with Rolling Stone. The singer, who has developed quite the reputation as a womanizer, told the magazine, "I mean, I'm quite happy. I'm happy in all aspects of my life. I'm very happy in all aspects of my life."

They both look pretty happy if you ask us.
