3 Dead in Reality Show Crash

Three reality television show workers died after a helicopter crashed in ranch in Acton, California, early Sunday morning. It's not yet known which reality show the men were working on, but the Los Angeles Film Commission reported a company called Bongo Productions was shooting in the area. Polsa Rosa Ranch is a popular setting for movie, TV and commercials shoots, and has two airstrips.
