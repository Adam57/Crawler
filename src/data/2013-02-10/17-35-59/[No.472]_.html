Editors' Picks

Editors’ Picks is a feature that allows publishers to showcase original, innovative news content that they have on their site at any given time. This content may include long-form narrative articles, slideshows, interactive graphics, or video stories.

The links you see in Editors’ Picks are hand-picked by the news organization whose logo is displayed above the links.

If you are a News publisher and wish to submit an Editors’ Picks feed to Google News, our guidelines and documentation provide further instruction.
