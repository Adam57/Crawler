Usher & Jennifer Hudson Party & Perform at Pre-Grammy Gala

Tweet
Posted Sunday February 10, 2013 5:03 PM GMT

Joining in on the weekend's festivities, Usher and Jennifer Hudson were on hand for the Pre-Grammy Gala on Saturday (February 9) in Beverly Hills.

Donning a short gold frock with a criss-crossed bodice, the "Dreamgirls" alum took to the stage to perform at the event as did the "My Way" crooner, who looked dashing in a handsome grey suit.

Before hitting the spotlight to entertain the eager audience, Hudson and Usher mingled with their fellow Hollywood celebs including Sean Diddy Combs and Miley Cyrus.

Held at the Beverly Hills Hilton Hotel, the evening was billed as a salute to industry icons and honoring L.A. Reid for his contributions to music.

The music industry's biggest night, the Grammy Awards air tonight (February 10) beginning at 8/7 central on CBS.
