Chris Brown Crashes Car, Blames Paparazzi: Report

Christina Lee
February 10th, 2013 // Leave a Comment
Tweet

More bad news for Breezy: Beverly Hills police say that Chris Brown crashed his Porsche into an alley wall at around noon Saturday (February 9). Brown told police that he lost control while trying to avoid paparazzi. Both police and a spokesman have verified that Brown escaped injury.

According to Brown’s spokesman, the R&B singer was on his way to an event when two vehicles cut him off, then paparazzi jumped out. “In an effort to remove himself from the situation, he began to back down an alley at which point his was cut off by two additional vehicles,” the spokesman said to E!. “Chris’ vehicle was totaled due to this aggressive pursuit by the paparazzi.”

Brown was last seen exiting a Los Angeles courthouse in regards to a possible parole violation. Red flags include his recent fight with Frank Ocean and, according to a Los Angeles County D.A., his submission of fraudulent community service records, which stemmed from the 2009 assault of his girlfriend Rihanna.
