Three dead in California copter crash 

The helicopter was reportedly part of an film production shoot. NTSB and FAA officials are conducting an investigation.

By Christine Roberts / NEW YORK DAILY NEWS

Published: Sunday, February 10, 2013, 10:39 AM
Updated: Sunday, February 10, 2013, 3:52 PM
Print
  • Print
  • Comment

KTLA5

Three people were killed in a helicopter crash Sunday morning in Acton, Calif. 

Three people died in a helicopter crash at an apparent film production shoot in California early Sunday morning.

The fatal crash occured just before 4 a.m. at Polsa Rosa Ranch in Acton, Calif., a small town in the rugged Sierra Pelona Mountains, a local ABC affiliate reported. Everybody on board died, Los Angeles County Fire dispatcher Robert Diaz said. 

A production company had permits to shoot an unnamed reality show on the private ranch, the Los Angeles Film Commission told KCAL-TV. Filming kicked off Saturday and included the use of the Bell 206 Jet Ranger chopper. 

KTLA5

The fatal crash reportedly occured at Polsa Rosa Ranch on Soledad Canyon Rd. 

The circumstances surrounding the deadly crash remain unclear. 

Officials from the National Transportation Safety Board and the Federal Aviation Administration are currently investigating the incident.
