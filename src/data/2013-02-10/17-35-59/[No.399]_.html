Katy Perry And John Mayer Match In Winter Whites At Pre-Grammy Party

Posted 1 hour ago by Chrissy Mahlmeister in Events, Grammys

Katy Perry and John Mayer at the Pre-Grammy Gala in L.A. on Feb. 9.
Photo: Getty Images

All together now: Awwwww! Last night at the Pre-Grammy Gala in L.A., cuter-than-cute couple Katy Perry and John Mayer matched in super fancy all-white ensembles, which we LOVE. KP wore a Rafael Cennamo fall 2013 gown, which was dripping in pearls, beads, and other incredibly ornate details, while John went for a more classic white tuxedo jacket, white top, and black trouser outfit. We're obsessed with his hand-tied bow tie and the gold button detailing on his shirt. Katy didn't need many accessories because, um, haiiii #pearlsgalore, but she sported pearl (duh) earrings and a giant cocktail ring to finish the look. For her beauty game, she wore a soft smokey eye with a shiny mauve lip and loose waves. PERF.

But, as you may remember, this isn't the first time Katy and John have dressed similar—last summer the (then maybe) couple was sporting the same boho chic bandelero hat with a button-up shirt and layered necklaces. <333 Well, it's just a few hours before the actual Grammys show starts, and we're anxious to see if they'll be all-out twins for that as well! Stay tuned into our live blog and red carpet photo gallery for constant style updates throughout the night!
