Justin Bieber brings the fever to SNL, says he's 'really sorry' for smoking marijuana

Bieber hosted and performed on Saturday Night Live paying homage to love and Black History Month.

By Zayda Rivera / NEW YORK DAILY NEWS

Sunday, February 10, 2013, 12:00 PM
Print
  • Print
  • Comment

NBC

Justin Bieber hosted Saturday Night Live on February 9.

Winter storm Nemo may have frozen New York City but Saturday Night Live had Bieber fever.

Justin Bieber hosted and performed on SNL paying tribute to Valentines Day and Black History Month.

Its not every year that Black History Month and Valentines Day fall in the same month, the 18-year-old heartthrob joked to an audience of screaming fans.

NBC

Comedian Kenan Thompson coached Bieber as he gave ladies in the audience roses and Black History Month 'facts.'

Before approaching the crowd, SNL comedian Kenan Thompson, 34, asked Bieber if he needed his, Black History Month flashcards.

JB quickly stated, I got this, before flirting with young women in the audience, giving them roses and stating random facts about Black History Month.

You know you sexy, he told one girl. But did you also know that Phillis Wheatley was the first black woman to publish a book of poems?

NBC

Justin Bieber gave comedian Whoopi Goldberg a bouquet of roses before inviting her on stage with him saying, 'Happy Valentine's Day Whoopi.'

He continued by adding not-so-factual information about the celebratory month like, Did you know that Denzel Washington invented the peanut? and Did you know that black folks invented the Kwanzaa?

He even serenaded the audience singing, Well baby did you know that Maya Angelou invented the peanut? before Thompson stopped him.

The last lady he approached was comedian Whoopi Goldberg who he presented a bouquet of roses to before inviting on stage.

NBC

Bieber played the role of Miley Cyrus' fan club president in the skit 'The Miley Cyrus Show' where he gave a third party apology for getting caught smoking marijuana.

You ready to celebrate with me? Justin asked her.

You better believe it, Whoopi responded.

Justin took part in a slew of skits throughout the night but one in particular stood out when he acted as the president of Miley Cyrus fan club on The Miley Cyrus Show, giving a third party apology for recently getting caught smoking marijuana.

Miley youre seriously my favorite singer of all time. Youre light years better than that douche Justin Bieber, he said. I heard he still has his baby teeth. I also heard he got busted for smoking weed and hes really sorry about it and that people make mistakes and hes never gonna do it again.

The singer was photographed smoking what appeared to be the illegal substance at a party inside a Newport Beach hotel room in California on January 2.

Miley, played by comedian Vanessa Bayer, responded, Yea right me too!
