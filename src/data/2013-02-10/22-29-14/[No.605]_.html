Megabus - Inter City Travel Bargains

Sunday February 10, 2013

Do train seats provide the cheapest way to get around Europe?

Many times, the answer is no.

For example, Megabus at times offers people who book online a trip between London and Paris for as little as $8. But to take advantage of that fare, you'll have to give up the heart of your day. Overnight trips come in at more money but offer time management advantages. Trains, which cost much more, also make the trip in less than half the time required of Megabus.

In North America, Megabus fares begin at $1 in some cases and serve about 70 cities. But it will take some additional investigation to find out if Megabus travel is best for your next trip.

Senior Discounts for Budget Travel

Sunday February 3, 2013

There are travel deals accorded only to senior adults. But the definition of "senior adult" might surprise you.

Some start age-related cost-cutting as young as age 50. Others list 55 as a "senior." For some reason, 62 is a popular cut-off point with other companies and travel providers.

I've actually received angry emails from readers who don't appreciate these definitions -- thinking they are my descriptions of a senior traveler.

At age 53, I don't think of myself as a senior traveler, but I'll be happy to accept any age-related discounts offered now or at any time in the future. Since I imagine most readers would take a similar stance, here are some Senior Discounts for Budget Travel across a number of travel categories.

Latin American Budget Travel Myths

Sunday February 3, 2013

The past few days, I have enjoyed the warmth and beauty of Puerto Vallarta, among the most popular destinations for international travelers to Mexico.

In the past few years, I've also visited Ecuador, Panama and Costa Rica. Each presents excellent opportunities for budget travelers, but many in our number are hesitant about exploring these places.

Careful planning and common-sense security precautions are important. But you must also avoid putting credence in budget travel myths for Latin America. Don't cheat yourself. Learn to make value-oriented destination choices.
