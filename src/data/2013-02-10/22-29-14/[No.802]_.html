William Benton Museum of Art Seeks Director

Sunday February 10, 2013

The William Benton Museum of Art at the University of Connecticut in Storrs, CT, is currently seeking to hire a new director.

The Museum is part of the University's School of Fine Arts. Requirements include having an advanced degree, at least 5 years museum and management experience.

Please refer to the online job application for further information.

Christie's Contemporary Art Sale

Sunday February 10, 2013

Peter Doig's 'The Architect's Home in the Ravine'. Photo by Peter Macdiarmid/Getty Images

Christie's will hold a Post War and Contemporary art sale with an evening sale on February 13, 2013 and a day sale on February 14, 2013.

Art auction houses like Christie's are great places to work if you enjoy fine art. Learn more about auction houses.

Slater Memorial Museum in Norwich, CT

Sunday February 10, 2013

The Slater Memorial Museum in Norwich, CT is located at the campus of the Norwich Free Academy.

The Museum is worth a visit as the architecture by Stephen C. Earle is a fine example of the Romanesque Revival style.

The Stamford Museum & Nature Center in CT

Sunday February 10, 2013
The Stamford Museum & Nature Center in Stamford, CT is located on the sprawling estate of the late Henri Bendel.

The Museum features art, natural and agricultural science, and history. They also post current job listings on their website.
