News & Press

  • Mayor Bloomberg and Borough President Markowitz Break Ground on Restoration of Historic Loew's Kings Theatre in Flatbush

    January 23, 2013

    Mayor Michael R. Bloomberg and Brooklyn Borough President Marty Markowitz today broke ground on the restoration of the historic Loew’s Kings Theatre in Flatbush, Brooklyn. Originally constructed in 1929, the 3,200-seat Loew’s Kings Theatre is the largest indoor theater in Brooklyn.

  • New York City Economic Development Corporation Announces Third Class of “NYC Venture Fellows”

    January 31, 2013

    NYCEDC and Fordham University today announced the newest class of NYC Venture Fellows, an annual, year-long program to help emerging entrepreneurs from around the world to grow or expand their businesses in New York through executive-level mentoring and exclusive networking opportunities.
