Welcome to the Bern Dibner Library!

This guide will give you an overview of what Dibner Library has to offer, including:

  • Thousands of books listed in our catalog, PolyCat 
  • Electronic journals and books available in our databases
  • Assistance from a librarian
  • Self help research guides
  • Study rooms
  • Printing and computing services
  • Research workshops
  • Primary sources in our special collections and archives

To ensure that everyone enjoys their library experience, make your self familiar with our policies.

What Will I Find in the Library?

Main Level:

  • Service Desk (reference librarians, course reserve materials, current newspapers and magazines, audio books and videos)
  • Photocopiers
  • Printers
  • Print journals and abstracts
  • Reference books
  • Computers in the atrium for searching the online catalog and internet
  • Computers in lab equipped with Microsoft Office and other software
  • Group study rooms
  • Reservable study rooms
  • Videocassette and DVD screening room
  • NYU-Poly archives
  • New and recommended books
  • Restrooms

Upper Level:

  • Books that you may borrow
  • Print indexes and abstracts
  • Individual study rooms
  • Group study rooms
  • Snack Lounge (LC 440)
  • Restrooms
