Mardi Gras

Saturday February 9, 2013

The words "Mardi Gras" mean Fat Tuesday. Mardi Gras is celebrated between February 3 and March 9 depending on the Lunar calendar and is always 47 days before Easter Sunday.

Random Acts of Kindness Week

Friday February 8, 2013

Image © Dixie Allan

The week of February 11 - 17, 2012 is Random Acts of Kindness Week. A random act of kindness is a selfless act performed by a person or people wishing to either assist or cheer up an individual person or people. I can tell you from personal experience that performing a selfless act of kindness for a total stranger brings you as much joy as it gives to the stranger. I have been doing this for several years now - just whenever the situation is suitable.

I hope you visit the link to the short article on this subject, you will find lots of thoughts on kindness from well-known people that will inspire you and hopefully you will make performing these selfless acts a part of your life. Enjoy!
