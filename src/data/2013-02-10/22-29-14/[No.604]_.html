Talking With Hometalk

Wednesday January 30, 2013

I recently stumbled across a fun site that appears similar to Pinterest but is reserved solely for home talk. In fact, the creators of this site named it exactly that: Hometalk.

But Hometalk goes way beyond pretty photos or cute quips. The creators of Hometalk say this... "Hometalk.com exists for one simple reason: to make home improvement better and easier for you." I like that.

On Hometalk, you can ask and answer questions about home and gardening, share and view project photos, and search and browse through previous discussions. Hometalk can also connect you with a pro, if needed. I like all that too.

So login and check it out, and get busy creating, celebrating and sharing your amazing home!
