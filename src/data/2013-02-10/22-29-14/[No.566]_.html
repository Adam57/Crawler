Save 60% on Fleece for Baby at Amazon.com

Friday December 14, 2012

Fleece is one of the best fabrics in the cooler months of the year. It keeps baby dry, even when he sweats, and it's a nice and easy layer that isn't too bulky.

And reducing bulk is important because you never want to compromise car seat safety.

Amazon is having a great sale on fleece and sweaters for babies right now and you don't want to miss it. This unisex Columbia baby and infant bear bunting is marked down from $45 and depending on what color you choose, you'll spend between $29 and $32 on it.

Not too bad. Especially because it's unisex ... remember, if you're planning on having more kids, unisex clothing is the way to go.

Friday November 23, 2012

So, maybe you're out elbowing the rest of the Black Friday shoppers ... or maybe you're hanging back to see what's available online for deals today and through the weekend.

Either way, I'll tell you what I've found: the deals online are basically the same as what was advertised in all the flyers, and that's no joke.
