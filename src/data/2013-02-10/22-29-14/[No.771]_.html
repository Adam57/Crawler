Host a Chocolate Theme Party

Friday February 8, 2013

I admit that Valentine's Day is the inspiration behind this party theme. Every single place I look there is chocolate. Of course, it would help if I wasn't always wandering around in stores like Godiva or haunting the chocolate shelves when I went food shopping. But, putting personal problems aside, it is the perfect time of year for a chocolate theme party. The chocolate doesn't melt at this time of year unless you really want it to in your fondue pot. Excuse me now, I hear a piece of chocolate calling to me from the kitchen.

Celebrate the Chinese New Year

Friday February 1, 2013

Long time readers of this site know that I like to take advantage of any good luck opportunity that comes my way. That's why, even though I'm not Chinese, I like to celebrate the Chinese New Year. Even if I wasn't looking for a little extra good luck, it's always nice to have an excuse to make another fresh start in the year. Join me in hosting a party to celebrate the Chinese New Year of the Black Water Snake which arrives on February 10.
