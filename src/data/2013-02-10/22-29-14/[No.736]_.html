USNS Cesar Chavez Delivered

Thursday January 31, 2013
General Dynamics has delivered the USNS Cesar Chavez dry cargo-ammunition ship. This ship is 689 feet long that can carry 10,000 tons of cargo (food, ammunition, dry goods and provisions) to other ships worldwide. The USNS Cesar Chavez is a key component in the global logistics system to keep our troops supplied.
