Plug In, Stream On

Sunday January 20, 2013

I listen to a lot of music when I'm traveling. But sometimes the cords from my earbuds to my iPhone can be a hassle. That's where the BlueAnt Ribbon comes in.

The BlueAnt Ribbon is a stereo Bluetooth music streamer. It's a small clip that connects via Bluetooth to an iPhone, Android phone, or other digital device. The clip has a headphone jack that allows the wearer to pug in an existing pair of earbuds, headphones, or the pair included in the package.
