Hair and Beauty Trade Shows 2013

Tuesday January 29, 2013

Some stylists fall victim to the idea that they've already been to school, completed their apprenticeship, and have now been working for several years over - so why more education? When we think we've learned everything, we run the risk of being left behind. As a beauty professional, there are constantly new techniques and emerging technologies to learn about. Now more than ever, product technology is advancing exponentially. It's our job to stay up to date.

Hair and Beauty trade shows are a great way to get inspired, which will help you retain the clients you already have, and gain new ones. So check out this list of hair and beauty trade shows for 2013 and start marking your calendars!

Would You Charge $1200 for a Hair Cut?

Friday December 21, 2012

Ted Gibson, the man behind the world's most expensive hair cut at $900, has upped his own ante and raised his price to $1200. What?! This decision has been praised in the press for elevating the value of our services, but personally I think it's a little nuts.  Trust me - I've stood next to Ted while he's performed one of his "experiences", as he calls them, and though the man can cut and style like the best of them, I see no logic in taking $1200 from someone just because you gave them a haircut and a really good head massage. Sorry Ted, I love you, but personally I think charging that much makes a joke out of our industry and our services. What do you think?
