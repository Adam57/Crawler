Smoke Inn's Abe Dababneh

Tuesday February 5, 2013

They say that Nestor Miranda of the Miami Cigar Company is the most interesting man in the cigar business, but the guy to really keep your eye on is Smoke Inn's Abe Dababneh. Abe is not only a successful cigar retailer, he is also the host of the annual Great Smoke cigar event in South Florida, an IPCPR Board Member, a cigar radio broadcaster, and is involved in many other aspects of the cigar industry. To find out what Abe is up to now, read Part I of my exclusive three-part Interview with Abe Dababneh of the Smoke Inn.

Cigar and Chocolate Pairings

Monday February 4, 2013

Think outside the candy box for Valentine's Day this year. Nat Sherman has suggested some cigar and chocolate pairings to make February 14, 2013 a little more special. Like excellent wine and handcrafted cigars, fine chocolate contains a tremendous range of flavors depending on the type, percentage of cacao, country of origin and additional ingredients.

"Cigars are a wonderful accompaniment to chocolate. Chocolate's density makes it able to stand up to even the strongest cigars," says Nat Sherman's cigar pairings expert, Michael Herklots.
