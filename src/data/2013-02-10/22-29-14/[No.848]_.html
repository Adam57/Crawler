Boy Scouts Delay Vote on Admitting Gays

Wednesday February 6, 2013

Just yesterday, it looked like the Boy Scouts of America might finally vote out their archaic rule banning gay scouts and scout leaders.

But as CNN reports:

"The board had been expected to vote Wednesday on a proposal to let local groups set their own policies, but said instead that it needs more time to get comment on the issue from its members."

According to the organization, that time won't be until at least May.
