Top Places To Sell Your Comic Books

Thursday January 31, 2013

For some, selling a comic book is something that is never considered. It is something tantamount to heresy for many comic book fans. There are some though that just luck into a stash of comics and want to get out quick. Where do you go to sell your comic books? Check out some of the best places to sell your comic books and then be ready to reap the rewards. You will need to make sure that you know the grade and value of your comics as this will give you an edge in the long run, but when you do, you will be able to figure out how much is the right price for your treasured collection.

Superheroes For Hospice

Monday January 28, 2013

It is always great when comic fans can help others out through the medium of comic books. There are many fantastic comic book based charities out there that comic fans can give to and they are all great ways to help others through comic books. Superheroes for Hospice is one of those organizations. People can give their unwanted comics to the organization and then Superheroes for Hospice goes into action. They categorize the comics and sell them at mini comic conventions in the New Jersey area. Creators have often been known to donate their time as well, making appearances at the event. Check out a profile of Superheroes for Hospice and see how you can help.

ComicLock - Extreme Comic Protection

Monday January 28, 2013

For the longest time, there has been little in the means of both displaying your high end comic books and keeping them safe. ComicLock seeks to change that with their comic display wall safe. The product serves dual purpose as a wall frame for your comic book and as a wall safe to keep unwanted elements out. The designers behind the ComicLock product have taken a lot of things into account in the making of this product and it looks like the real deal. I got a chance to see one up close and personal and reviewed the ComicLock display and storage system. Check it out and see if it might just be for you.
