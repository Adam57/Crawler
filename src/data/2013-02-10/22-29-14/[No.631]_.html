Quest For The Rainbow Bridge

Saturday February 9, 2013

It is not surprising that several authors have laid claim to writing the "original" Rainbow Bridge poem. I discovered this fact several years ago and went on a Quest to discover the true origin of The Rainbow Bridge poem.


The Rainbow Bridge poem has comforted me on several occasions after losing one of my beloved cat, as I know it has done for others. It is hard to keepa dry eyey no matter how many times we have read it, because we all have one or more "Rainbow Kitties and Doggies" we envision there.
Photo Credit: © iStockPhoto/Borut Trdina

We may never learn who is the real author of The Rainbow Bridge, but one true thing rings clear: This sweet story will continue to bring peace and comfort to thousands of animal lovers through the ages to come. And I know in my heart that one-by-one, we will some day be reunited with our loved cats and dogs who have gone before.

Which is the Best Pet: A Cat or a Dog?

Friday February 8, 2013

We Pets Guides have gotten together for some friendly rivalry, and are each featuring a special on our site about the comparative value of cats versus dogs as pets. My contribution is 10 Reasons Why Cats Are Better Than Dogs.


Image © iStock Photo / Liliya Kulianionak

Don't get me wrong; I like dogs - when I was growing up my family always had dogs. Although I had one or two cats, I wasn't really crazy about them until the boy I married introduced me to them. Today, if my living circumstances were different, I'd add a dog to our mix - maybe one like the cute little Pug featured here. But our four cats have never been around dogs and would never tolerate it. So all-in-all, since I have to choose, I absolutely think that cats make better pets than dogs. About that "pets" thing - most of us consider our cats as family members, rather than pets, so we're only using that word for brevity.

Cat Lovers, We're Outnumbered!

The competition is off and running as Amy Shojai, About.com's Puppies Guide, has contributed her fun piece, 10 Reasons Puppies Are Better than Cats. Jenna Stregowski, About.com Guide to Dogs, makes some pretty convincing argument for d*gs too: 10 Reasons Dogs Are Better Than Cats. Your supportive comments here on my blog are welcome - I'm sure you'll come up with many more reasons, and you're also welcome to comment on the Puppies blog, and/or the Dogs blog.

Things Cats Hate

Thursday February 7, 2013

Does your cat seem to be upset all the time lately, but you can't figure out why? Perhaps this list of things cats hate will provide a clue.

Cats' Top Pet Peeves

It goes without saying that we all want our cats to be happy, and it's as upsetting to us as it is to them when they're not. Even the mildest-mannered cats can become grumpy when their world is in disarray. Cats are meticulous and want their surroundings to be just so, and "almost" just doesn't get it. Take an "almost clean" litter box. Would you want to use an "almost clean" public toilet? Cats don't, either.

It may surprise you to find out exactly why this cat is so angry, and it may not be what you suspect. Check out these things cats hate, and examine your cat parenting skills.
