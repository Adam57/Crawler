2013 Epcot Flower & Garden Festival

Wednesday January 30, 2013

There is usually some foot-dragging involved when I tell my children we're going to visit a botanical garden, but I suspect this wouldn't be the case if we went to the fanciful Epcot International Flower & Garden Festival. The 2013 festival runs from March 6 to May 19 and features themed garden displays, Disney topiaries, and educational garden presentations. When the family is flowered out, you can enjoy the Epcot park, as the festival requires valid park admission.

2013 Wedding Flower Trends

Sunday January 27, 2013

Feeling overwhelmed about choosing wedding flowers? The wedding flower wholesaler FiftyFlowers.com shares 2013 trends to help brides hone in on their best blooms:

  • Full, lush flowers (like peonies and hydrangeas)
  • Large footed vases and urns
  • Romantic pastel palettes
  • Among the most sought-after flowers: celosia

Ashikaga Flower Park

Thursday January 24, 2013

If you've ever been captivated by a mature wisteria vine in full bloom, you should add a visit to the Ashikaga Flower Park in Japan to your bucket list. Although the park features seasonal flowers throughout the year, the Japanese wisteria that peaks from mid April to mid May is astonishing. The massive trellises and tunnels covered in a cascading drapery of purple, white, and pink blooms are unreasonably beautiful. Plan to get a massage afterwards for the neck ache you'll have after gaping at the overhead display for several hours.
