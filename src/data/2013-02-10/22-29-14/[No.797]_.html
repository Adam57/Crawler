Watch U.S.and Canadian Nationals and European Figure Skating Online

Tuesday January 22, 2013

Ice skating fans can watch their favorite figure skaters compete in national and international figure skating competitions from the comfort of their computers, iPads, Kindles, and other electronic devices on icenetwork, Universal Sports, or on Canada's Skate Buzz.

The 2013 European Figure Skating Championships will be available via live stream on Universal Sports from from January 24 through January 26, 2013. The 2013 Canadian Figure Skating Championships took place from January 13 through January 20, and the US Figure Skating Championships is going on from January 20 through January 27, 2013.

It's really fun to watch these competitions online.  Viewers get the "best seat in the house!"
