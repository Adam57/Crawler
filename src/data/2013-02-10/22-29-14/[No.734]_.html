Superbowl Weekend in Detroit

Wednesday January 30, 2013

Whether you head out to a sports bar to watch the game or cater in, there are several options in and around Detroit on Superbowl Sunday. There are also some interesting Detroit connections to the Superbowl in 2013, including players on the San Francisco 49ers and a list of years the Detroit Lions won the NFL Championship.

If football isn't your thing, check out one of the several concerts, plays and expos in town this weekend.

Must See at 2013 Detroit Auto Show: Tesla Model X

Sunday January 20, 2013

Tesla Model X at 2013 Detroit Auto Show © Bill Pugliano / Getty Images

While the Tesla Model X was unveiled in early 2012, deliveries don't begin until 2014 so you can still get a thrill by checking it out at the 2013 Detroit Auto Show.

The Model X draws the eye right away with its Falcon wing doors, but it manages to retain interest as you consider the possibilities of a seven-seat electric vehicle with a lower center of gravity than those other SUVs or Minivans. The Model X also has a state-of-the-art Tesla Touchscreen and a Dual Motor AWD option that will allow the car to accelerate from 0 to 60 MPH in less than five seconds.
