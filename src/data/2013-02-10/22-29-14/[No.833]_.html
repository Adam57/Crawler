Free Pancake Day

Monday February 4, 2013

It's that time again ... stop by IHOP tomorrow (February 5), and get a free short stack. All they ask is that you consider making a donation to Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. Since starting National Pancake Day in 2006, they've raised more than 10 million dollars for charity. Impressive, eh?
