Sweetheart Sips: Valentine's Day Cocktail

Sunday February 10, 2013

There are many great restaurants in the world and Makoto is one that I've heard nothing but praise for. Their innovative Japanese dishes are often accompanied by creative cocktails such as this one and if you happen to be near Bal Harbour, Florida the weekend of Valentine's Day 2013, this will be one of the cocktail options you will find. For the rest of us, well we can make this Valentine's Day Cocktail at home.


Valentine's Day Cocktail

Today's feature starts with Cazadores Tequila, which remains a personal favorite, especially the smooth and sweet reposado. The cool part of this drink is that they have paired the tequila with Chartreuse and muddle both a serrano pepper and blood orange for their spice and juice respectively. Add a little sweet, sour, and bitter, now how fun is that combination? Get the Valentine's Day Cocktail recipe...

Sweetheart Sips: Happily Ever After

Saturday February 9, 2013

When it comes to unique tastes in this round of Sweetheart Sips, the Happily Ever After is the winner and it is nothing short of a fabulous drink that will leave you with a smile on your face.


Happily Ever After

The flavor combination in this cocktail is definitely out of the ordinary and that is why I love it and encourage anyone who is out for an adventure in drinks to try. It begins with the source, Rhum J.M. Blanc, a rhum agricole made from sugar cane rather that molasses. This sweet base is then compounded with Cynar (an artichoke-dominant bitter), Cherry Heering, Averna (a herbal digestif) and not one, but two bitters - rhubarb and whiskey barrel. The combined taste is one that is really hard to describe and you just have to try it for yourself. Get the Happily Ever After cocktail recipe...

Sweetheart Sips: Clementine Crush

Friday February 8, 2013

Grand Lux Cafe has locations around the United States and today's sweetheart sipper is a regular on their menus. The Clementine Crush is an adventure in fruit with one little twist and the presentation is perfect for sharing with your crush on Valentine's Day.


Clementine Crush Cocktail

The mix is one of Svedka Clementine Vodka, Cointreau, pineapple, lime, and passion fruit, though before all of that you will muddle ginger with simple syrup. That element of sweet with the zingy spice of ginger is one of the great aspects of this cocktail and it works unbelievably well with the fruit-vodka punch. The other delight here is the clementine garnish which is against the inside of the glass and it is an idea that can be used in a number of other cocktails. Get the Clementine Crush recipe...
