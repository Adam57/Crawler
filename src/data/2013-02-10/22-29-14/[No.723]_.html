Major Days Shocker: Kate and Rafe Have Sex

Friday February 8, 2013

Two unlikely Salemites hit the sheets, next week. In a major Days of Our Lives shocker, Kate and Rafe have sex.

The new issue of Soap Opera Digest details the events leading up to the unexpected encounter. Kate, down, out and angry after a phone conversation with Stefano, heads to a bar for a drink. There she meets Rafe, who's angry and on the rebound after losing Sami to EJ.

They drink. They commiserate. They discuss the Will/Gabi situation... It all leads to a steamy night of passion.

It's not the first time Kate has bedded one of Sami's men. Remember she actually had an affair with EJ, before he became involved with Sami.

But it is the first time Rafe has bedded one of Sami's enemies and EJ's exes. Although there were sparks between him and Nicole, the twosome decided to remain friends.

To read Lauren Koslow's take on Kate and Rafe's steamy night and what happens the next morning, pick up the latest issue of Soap Opera Digest.

Drake Hogestyn, Deidre Hall Reflect on Their Days Love Story

Friday February 8, 2013

Days of Our Lives viewers are as enamored with John and Marlena as the twosome are with each other.

In honor of their longtime super coupling, Drake Hogestyn and Deidre Hall reflect on the working relationship and friendship they've shared for over two decades in the new issue of Soap Opera Digest.

Among the subjects they discuss is the strong response their paring has elicited from fans.

"Deidre always says we put them in a warm fuzzy place," explains Hogestyn, noting that he and Hall are "safety nets for each other" and "never try to upstage each other."

Hall weighs in on how she and Hogestyn keep their screen romance fresh after all these years. "I'm really comfortable doing anything with Drake in the creative process," shares the actress. "When Drake and I are doing a scene, sometimes I have no idea what he is going to do -- whether it's pick me up or whatever."

To read their entire interview, pick up the new issue of Soap Opera Digest.
