SF Beer Week

Monday January 28, 2013

There's lots of attention to romance in February, but hey, beer can be romantic too. (Just ask anyone at a really good kegger.)  SF Beer Week attracts a different kind of beer fan -- the kind that that's interested in quality first -- and then quantity. There's both at this event, which has more than 300 craft and microbrew-focused events in the San Francisco Bay Area.

And learn more about how to plan your travels around beer here.

Wine and Chocolate: A Terrible Couple?
