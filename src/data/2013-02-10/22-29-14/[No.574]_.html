Data, A Love Story by Amy Webb

Wednesday January 30, 2013

Out this week, a true story about a data analyst who applied her analytical skills to online dating to figure out how to get more matches and choice people who would be a good fit for herself from among them. She met her husband this way and now shares her story and tips in Data, A Love Story. If you have tried online dating, or are considering it, are you intrigued by Amy Webb's offer of analytical advice?
