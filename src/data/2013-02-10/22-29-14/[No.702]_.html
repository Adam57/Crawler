From Keller to Moonen, Mandalay Bay Offers Top Chefs Aplenty

Wednesday November 30, 2011

Hubert Keller. Rick Moonen. Michael Mina. Wolfgang Puck. If the best of the best is a must when planning your culinary events in Las Vegas, Mandalay Bay has them all under one roof. And, when you add unique dining venues that include dining next to a lagoon filled with swans? Even more amazing.

When it Comes to Group Dining, Planners Will Find Devilish Fun at Monte Carlo

Wednesday November 30, 2011

Imagine your group or special event taking place just steps from the famed Las Vegas Strip with views up and down the famous promenade. Imagine your group or special event taking place under the Vegas stars with live entertainment a cinch. Imagine a raucous good time at one of the hottest Mexican restaurants. Are you there yet? No, not yet. But, a quick call to Diablo's Cantina at Monte Carlo in Las Vegas should get you there a whole lot sooner.

Group Dining Venues To Heat up Your Special Event at The Mirage

Wednesday November 30, 2011

Just as hot as its fiery Volcano are the popular restaurants found within The Mirage in Las Vegas. From an outpost by B.B. King himself (often featuring live acts) to the burger joint of a world-renowned chef, this Las Vegas resort is more than able to satisfy the cravings of even the hungriest of business travelers.

Group Dining is King at MGM Grand in Las Vegas

Wednesday November 30, 2011

There's rumors that MGM Grand is on the verge of shaking up the Vegas dining scene, but what it provides to groups now continues to be in high demand. Meeting planners will find not one, but two outposts by the world-renowned Chef of the Century Joel Robuchon as well as fine dining experiences by Wolfgang Puck, Michael Mina, Tom Colicchio and more.
