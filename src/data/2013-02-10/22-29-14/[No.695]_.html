Subway's Sweepstakes Gives Away 2 Cars and Over 2 Million Other Prizes

Sunday February 10, 2013
Subway is giving you an additional incentive to eat at their restaurants. Enter the Subway - Footlong Frenzy Instant Win Game and Sweepstakes and you could win one of two Jeep vehicles or over two million other prizes.

To enter, you'll need a code that you can get by buying participating products at Subway, by mail, or after February 23rd, online. You can also get codes through other methods, like online ads. Depending on which ads you enter when, or other actions you take, you "advance" the level of prizes that you can win.

The lowest level prizes include My Coke Rewards gift cards and $2 Subway gift cards. The next level of prizes include free Footlong Subs, free subs for a year, and $250 Southwest gift cards. And the top level of prizes include $1,000 Visa gift cards, free airfare vouchers for a year, free round-trip airfare, and 2013 Jeep Compasses. The prizes are worth up to $19,395.

Entry into Subway's Footlong Frenzy Instant Win Game and Sweepstakes is open to US residents aged 13 and up. Enter up to three times daily through March 8, 2013. Read more...

Want to Pop the Most Epic Propsal Ever? This Contest Could Help

Sunday February 10, 2013
You know, some people are happy with a quiet wedding proposal between two people. But if that's not you, describe your ultimate wedding proposal idea that takes place at the Iberostar Grand Hotel Paraiso resort and you could win a trip to make that proposal come true from Zales' Most Epic Proposal Ever Contest.

To enter, ubmit a video of two minutes in length of less describing your ideal surprise marriage proposal.

The grand-prize winner will receive a trip for two to the all-inclusive Iberostar Grand Hotel Paraiso Resort in Riviera Maya, Mexico to ask their sweetheart to marry them, a diamond engagement ring from the Zales Celebration Fire Collection, and a pair of wedding bands. The prize is worth $19,304.97.

Entry into Zales' Most Epic Proposal Ever Contest is open to US residents who are not legally married or engaged and who are at least 18 years old. Enter once before February 28, 2013. Read more...

Share Why You're "Pretty Amazing" to Win $10,000 and More from Seventeen's Contest

Sunday February 10, 2013
How would you like to not only appear on the cover of Seventeen Magazine, but also to win a scholarship for your education? Enter Seventeen's Pretty Amazing Contest 2013 for your chance to win that or other prizes.

To enter, submit your essay describing why you're pretty amazing along with recent photos of yourself.

The grand-prize winner will receive a $10,000 scholarship and an appearance on the cover of Seventeen. Five finalists will also receive a trip to NYC or another location selected by the sponsor to compete for the grand prize.

Entry into Seventeen's Pretty Amazing Contest 2013 is open to female residents of the US, Puerto Rico, or Canada (void in Quebec) who are between the ages of 15 and 22. Enter once before April 30, 2013. Read more...

Go On a Free Caribbean Vacation with PureWow's Sweepstakes

Sunday February 10, 2013
This time of year, (like most times of the year, really), a trip to the Caribbean sounds pretty good to me. And if you enter PureWow's Cure Your Winter Blues Giveaway, you could win a free trip to the British-owned island of Anguilla.

The grand-prize winner will receive a trip for two to Anguilla. The prize includes airfare, a stay in a luxurious room at the Viceroy Anguilla, a poolside cabana, resort credits, and more. Plus, the winner will receive $2,000 to spend at Club Monaco. The prize is worth $4,999.

Entry into PureWow's Cure Your Winter Blues Giveaway is open to US residents aged 21 and up. Enter once before February 17, 2013. Read more...
