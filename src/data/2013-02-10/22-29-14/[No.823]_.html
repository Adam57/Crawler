Free Sample of Gevalia Coffee K-Cups

Friday February 8, 2013

There's a offer available for a free sample of Gevalia coffee K-cups.

To get your free sample of Gevalia coffee K-cups you'll need to like Gevalia on Facebook and choose that you brew your morning coffee from a K-cup. Submit your mailing information and look for your free sample in 6-8 weeks.
