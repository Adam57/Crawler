Renninger's Antique Extravaganza

Sunday February 10, 2013

Three times a year they come — some by car and still others by the busloads — because the word is out... in the world of antiques, Renninger's rocks!

You can shop Renninger's Twin Markets any weekend to find antiques and flea market bargains, but three times a year over 1400 antique and collectible dealers come together for the three-day Renninger's Antique Extravaganza. You'll have to get there early to catch the 8:00 a.m. auction, but by 10:00 all the dealers — 400 inside and over 1,000 outside — are open for business. Some even save never-before-offered merchandise just for these extravaganzas, so you'll always find something new. Mark your calendar now and don't miss it!

  • What: Renninger's Antique Extravaganza
  • Where: Renninger's Twin Markets - Mt. Dora
  • When: February 15, 16 & 17, 2013

Renninger's Twin Markets are located in picturesque Mount Dora, about 20 miles northwest of Orlando on U.S. Highway 441, and Mount Dora is a lovely place to spend a day or a few days just about any time of the year.

Bands, Brew & BBQ Sizzles This Weekend

Thursday February 7, 2013

Beginning this Saturday and Sunday, guests of SeaWorld Orlando and Busch Gardens Tampa Bay can look forward to sizzling weekends of live performances, smokin' hot barbecue, ice-cold beer and thrilling attractions as Bands, Brew & BBQ gets underway. What a great recipe for a family getaway!

  • What: Bands, Brew & BBQ
  • When: Saturdays & Sundays, February 9 - March 17
  • Where: Saturdays & Sundays (Feb 9 & 10, 16 & 17, 23 & 24, Mar 2 & 3, 9 & 10, 16 & 17) at SeaWorld Orlando

Florida Mardi Gras Celebrations

Tuesday February 5, 2013

If it's February, it's time for Mardi Gras in Florida and this weekend kicks off most of the celebrations. Of course, Universal Orlando's Mardi Gras has become one of the most sought after tickets in O town since it features some of the hottest musical performers in addition to a parade that is as impressive as that in N'awlins. In addition, there are several community-based Mardi Gras celebrations that feature parades with colorful floats. One thing is certain, they all seem to have one thing in common... BEADS! Lots and lots of beads!

Discover the Fun at Florida State Fair

Tuesday February 5, 2013

It's time for the fair! The Florida State Fair opens its gates this Thursday, February 7, in Tampa.

It's the best time of the year to "Discover the Fun" at the Florida State Fair! The fair does have it going on... whether it's pig races, Elvis impersonators, cloggers or concerts. Of course, let's not forget the gut-wrenching rides, corn dogs, cotton candy and things fried that you never thought could be fried, and you've got a great day of fun. The fair runs through Monday, February 18, 2013.

Free and discounted admission is offered on the following days:

  • Thursday, February 7 - Opening day is "Hero's Day," with all active, reserve and retired military veterans, law enforcement, firemen and first responders admitted FREE with valid identification.
  • Friday, February 8 - "Student Day" is where all Hillsborough County students receive a free ticket for admission. Also, bring an empty Diet Mountain Dew can and save $5 off the regular price of a Midway ride armband.
  • Saturday, February 9 & 16 - All 4-H and FFA members admitted free!
  • Monday thru Thursday, February 11-14 - Senior Days admit seniors ages 55+ for $8 at the gate or $6 in advance online or at the Fairgrounds Box Office.
  • Monday, February 18 - "Student Day" for students in counties surrounding Hillsborough County to receive free admission. Also, bring an empty Diet Mountain Dew can and save $5 off the regular price of a Midway ride armband.

Concert lineup:

  • Monday, Feburary 11 - Starship featuring Mickey Thomas Concert at 7:30 p.m. in the Entertainment Hall. ($15 VIP seating, $10 reserved seating and limited FREE Mezzanine level bleacher seating.)
  • Tuesday, February 12 - Bellamy Brothers Concert at 7:30 p.m. in the Entertainment Hall. ($15 VIP seating, $10 reserved seating and limited FREE Mezzanine level bleacher seating.)
  • Wednesday, February 13 - Country Gold Tour at Noon and 4:00 p.m. in the Entertainment Hall. ($15 VIP seating, $10 reserved seating.)
  • Thursday, February 14 - Less Than Jake at 7:30PM in the Entertainment Hall. ($15 VIP seating, $10 reserved seating and limited FREE Mezzanine level bleacher seating.)
  • Monday, February 18 - LeAnn Rimes Concert at 7:30PM in the Entertainment Hall. ($35 VIP seating, $30 premium reserved seating and $25 reserved seating.)

What: Florida State Fair
When: February 7-18, 2013
Where: Florida State Fairgrounds, Tampa
