Valentine's Day Freebies

Tuesday January 29, 2013
The Valentine's Day promotions are slowly beginning to roll in. Here is what we have so far so mark your calendars.

Free Cookie at Quiznos
Quiznos has posted a printable coupon for a free cookie on Valentine's Day. It is valid on Feb. 14, 2013 only and at participating locations. The coupon has the list of areas where it can not be used.

Free Donuts at Krispy Kreme
Go to participating Krispy Kreme restaurants and purchase a dozen doughnuts and in return you will receive 12 free Valentines that have a coupon for a free Krispy Kreme doughnut inside.
