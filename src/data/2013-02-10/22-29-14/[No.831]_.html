Fishing Muddy Water

Sunday February 10, 2013
Fish will eat in muddy water but they are harder to catch. And cold muddy water, like we often have here in Georgia this time of year, makes it even more difficult. There are some things you can do, from finding the clearest water on the lake to using a loud bait, to improve your odds.

Inactive Winter Bass

Friday February 8, 2013
Catching bass in the winter is tough. I have a rule of thumb about water temperatures. If the temperature is above 50 degrees F I am confident I can catch bass. If it is between 45 and 50 my hope fades but I think I might get lucky and catch one. If the water temperature is below 45 I don't have much hope at all.

Tennessee Handle on a Fishing Rod

Thursday February 7, 2013
Tennessee handles on spinning rods are very popular. There is no real seat or mechanical device for attaching the reel. You just tape the reel to the rod handle. This saves weight and you can place the reel anywhere on the handle for balance.
