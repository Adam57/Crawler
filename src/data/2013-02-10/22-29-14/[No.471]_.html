January's Cool Thing: Legendary Monsters

Thursday January 31, 2013

February is typically when all the cool things hit the action figure industry, due to New York Toy Fair, but this month surprised me thanks to an incredibly fun new Kickstarter project called Legendary Monsters. This independent action figure line launch is seeking crowd-funding in order to produce a creative line of figures based on monsters of folklore like the Chupacabra or Mothman.

The designs are fresh and intriguing, the found has loads of industry experience, and the details of the project seem to be well considered and fulfilling to any subscribers. Legendary Monsters puts all the right pieces together for an independent action figure line, and it's almost scary how cool the line could be in the long run.

I encourage you to check out Legendary Monsters on Kickstarter, pledge if it looks interesting to you, and let a friend or two know about the project too. You won't want to miss the opportunity to pose and display these figures on your shelf.
