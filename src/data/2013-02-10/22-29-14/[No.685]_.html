Upgraded VIA APC Models Released

Monday January 28, 2013

The VIA APC development board, released in May2012, has an updated model. The new model has an improved processor, more memory, better video output and additional expansion options. The updated board comes in two forms, the $79 Rock which includes an VGA output, and the $99 Paper which includes a book-like cardboard case but drops the VGA connector leaving HDMI as the only video output. More information can be found at The Register or on VIA's APC website.

New Raspberry Pi Model Possible Next Year in 2014

Sunday January 27, 2013

The Raspberry Pi has been a breakthrough success with sales soaring past 1 million units in the first year of sales. The success of the platform has been followed with interest and calls for a new model from enthusiasts. Official word is that while an updated platform is in the works, it looks like it won't hit the market until 2014. Read more at about the Raspberry Pi's future here.
