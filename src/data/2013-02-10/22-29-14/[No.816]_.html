Is Super Bowl MVP Joe Flacco Worthy of a Super Contract?

Friday February 8, 2013

Is there anything in sports hotter than a winning Super Bowl quarterback?

And if that winning quarterback happens to be the MVP for the winning team? Man, he's so hot he's contributing to global warming. You can actually spot him from Google Earth.

Big contracts, beautiful women, huge endorsements - it's enough to give anybody a big ol' head.

That's Joe Flacco, currently the quarterback for the Baltimore Ravens, who, you know, won the Super Bowl.

People are saying he's one of the league's elite QBs now. His teammates are saying he's the best.

His agent is saying: Big Money. At least as much as Peyton Manning and Drew Brees.

Flacco is also, you see, a free agent. Nice timing, huh?

But, is Flacco really worth being paid a whopping contract bigger than the accepted elite NFL quarterbacks?

In a word: No.

2013 Super Bowl: Wisdom and Experience over Youth and Talent

Monday February 4, 2013

It seemed that the Young and Restless had everything  happening over the Old and Listless going into the 2013 Super Bowl or, more formally Super Bowl XLVII.

The San Francisco 49ers had tatooed quarterback Colin Kaepernick on a hot streak. The Baltimore Ravens had linebacker Ray Lewis going out on his retirement party.

There were fireworks aplenty in New Orleans last night and the youngsters did indeed put on a show. Kaepernick put up some good numbers in a 34-31 losing effort and Ravens' quarterback Joe Flacco had a super game, winning MVP honors.

But, when crunch time came, it was Baltimore's veterans who staved off defeat and handed the Ravens their second Lombardi Trophy, one more than the old Colts could manage for the city. Read all about it here.
