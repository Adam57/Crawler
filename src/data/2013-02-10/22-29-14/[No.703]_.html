Jason Aldean at No.1 with 'The Only Way I Know'

Thursday February 7, 2013

Jason Aldean's "The Only Way I Know" is a hit with radio listeners --  with some help from Luke Bryan and Eric Church.

Aldean's duet with the two singers rung in at #1 this week on the Country Airplay chart.

But the posse cut didn't have as much traction on the singles chart, where it couldn't edge past Blake Shelton's "Sure Be Cool If You Did."

Country Music Festivals Tune Up For Spring

Wednesday February 6, 2013

It's not too early to start thinking about 2013's country music festivals.

The big summer festivals have just started releasing their lineups. And there are no shortage of festivals locked-in for spring.

The most notable among them is California's Stagecoach Music Festival -- country music's answer to Coachella -- which runs from April 26 to 28 in Indio, California. The roster of singers includes everyone from Justin Townes Earle and Jeff Bridges to Toby Keith and Lonestar. Get your tickets now; it's sure to sell out.

Gary Allan Takes the Country Charts by 'Storm'

Friday February 1, 2013

Gary Allan has a lot to be happy about.

His brand-new album, Set You Free, debuted at #1 on the country album charts -- a turn of events that's certainly less delightful for Taylor Swift.

Joining Set You Free at the top of the charts is his song "Every Storm (Runs Out of Rain)." It pushed past The Band Perry's "Better Dig Two" to become Allan's fourth #1 country song, his first since 2004's "Nothing On But the Radio."

For all the info, check out this week's country music charts.

Johnny Cash -- the Postal Stamp!

Wednesday January 30, 2013

After releasing Miles Davis and Edith Piaf stamps in 2012, the United States Postal Service has seen fit to honor Johnny Cash with his own 46 cents of fame.

The first class stamp, which will be released later this year, features an image of Cash taken from the photo shoot for his iconic Ring of Fire: The Best of Johnny Cash.

Get started writing your tear-stained letters.
