Central America Photo for February: A Perfect Shore

Thursday February 7, 2013

Our Central America Photo for February features the Honduras Marine Reserve, located in southern Belize near -- you guessed it! -- Honduras. If you're hoping to spot a manatee on your trip to Belize, the Honduras Marine Reserve is a good bet. It includes 135 mangrove islands; a couple in particular are fantastic for swimming, with smooth, soft sand and delightfully warm water. I mean, look at that picture. Don't you just want to jump in?

Placencia Photo Gallery

Thursday January 31, 2013

Placencia, Belize is the Caribbean country's second most popular destination for travelers, after San Pedro Town on Ambergris Caye. The Placencia Peninsula is home to Belize's best mainland beaches, with a number of new resorts and hotels making the most of the soft, pale sands on the coastline. The town of Placencia itself is tiny and quirky -- a perfect example of the laid-back vibe Belize is famous for.

Central America Animal for January: Blue Morpho

Monday January 28, 2013

Our Central America animal for January is the Blue Morpho Butterfly, undoubtedly one of Central America's most beautiful creatures. I've been lucky enough to glimpse them in forests all over the isthmus. This particular butterfly came from the butterfly farm at the Lodge at Chaa Creek, a hotel in Belize's western Cayo region. The whole Blue Morpho butterfly life cycle (from egg to adult) is just 115 days.
