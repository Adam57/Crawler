Valentine's Day Arts and Crafts Projects

Wednesday February 6, 2013

Looking for some Valentine's Day arts and crafts projects?  I've been doing research about using Pinterest to promote your arts and crafts business (article will be up in the next few weeks), and found this pinboard with quite a few Valentine Day projects.

I've posted instructions for making goats' milk soap. It's quick and easy to make - add rose otto or jasmine essential oil to give it a Valentine's Day flair.  Jasmine is used to make my  Aphrodisiac Soap (here's the recipe).

Jasmine is an absolute not an essential oil.    If you use jasmine absolute,  make sure it is mascerated from  jasmine flowers and mixed with a good oil. Don't use some cheap synthetic.
