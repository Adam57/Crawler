Waterproof Samsung W300 Includes Dual Camera Capabilities

Sunday February 10, 2013

The look of one of Samsung's latest dual cameras may remind you more of a cell phone than a digital camera/camcorder, but it offers quite a few features that will set it apart from any cell phone.

The Samsung W300 pocket dual camera can shoot still images at up to 5.5MP of resolution and HD video at full 1080p. This camera has no optical zoom lens, and it offers a 2.3-inch LCD screen.

You also can shoot underwater with the W300, which works in up to 16 feet of water depth. It can survive a fall up to 6.5 feet as well.

The W300 is a dark gray dual camera, with black, red, or orange trim. Although the W300 carries an MSRP of $159.99, you certainly can find it for quite a bit less than that right now if you shop around. (Compare Prices)

Nikon's Latest Medium Telephoto Lens Works at f/1.8

Saturday February 9, 2013

Nikon recently began offering a powerful medium telephoto lens, the 85mm f/1.8G, for its DSLR cameras.

The medium telephoto 85mm f/1.8G lens offers an aperture range of between f/1.8 and f/16. It works with both autofocus and manual focus, and it has a focal length of 85mm.

The 85mm f/1.8G lens weighs about 12.4 ounces, and it carries a starting price of around $550. (Compare Prices)

Sony Bloggie Sport Provides Waterproof Option

Thursday February 7, 2013

Sony's Bloggie Sport waterproof camera will provide users with basic still image and video capabilities. It's great for shooting in rugged environments.

The Bloggie Sport, also called the MHS-TS22, can shoot full 1080p HD video, as well as 5MP still images. It includes a 2.7-inch touch screen LCD, and it has 4GB of internal memory. The Bloggie Sport has no memory card slot.

This camera, which is safe to use in up to 16 feet of water depth, carries an MSRP of $179.99. (Compare Prices) You can find it in black, blue, or red camera bodies.
