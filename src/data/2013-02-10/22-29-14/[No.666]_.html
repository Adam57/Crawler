"Toi qui mis la pitié" Lyrics and Translation

Thursday January 31, 2013

Here's another great aria from Massenet's opera, Thais - "Toi qui mis la pitié." Sung by the abstinent monk, Athanael, he finally learns of his task from God.  He will convert the beautiful seductress, Thais, to Christianity.  Learn the lyrics to "Toi qui mis la pitie," and follow along with this YouTube clip.

"O messager de Dieu" Lyrics and Translation

Thursday January 31, 2013

Here's a beautiful aria from Jules Massenet's opera, Thais. Sung in the first scene of act three, Thais sings of her thankfulness to the monk who brought her to salvation, even though he was rather rude to her.  Finally, he warms up to her when he sees that she has obeyed him despite her injuries acquired on their journey across the desert.  Learn the lyrics to "O messager de Dieu."

A Synopsis of Massenet's Opera, Thais

Sunday January 27, 2013

Massenet's opera, Thais, is one of my favorites.  Many of you, no matter  how familiar you are with classical music, have most assuredly heard one of the opera's most famous pieces, "Meditation."  Listen to a YouTube clip of Yo-Yo Ma performing this sublime piece of music.  Thais is a heart-wrenching opera that tells the story of a monk determined to convert a beautiful Venusian priestess to Christianity, and in the process, he falls in love with her.  Though, I'm sure you can guess the outcome, learn the synopsis of Massenet's opera, Thais.

The Rake's Progress Opera Synopsis

Sunday January 27, 2013

One of the few 20th century operas in standard operatic repertory, Igor Stravinsky's The Rake's Progress is a three act opera loosely based on the famous eight paintings by William Hogarth, portraying the fall of man after being persuaded by the devil into a life of indulgence, decadence, and deceit.   Though the opera originally was set to 18th century London, you will find it performed in many different settings - the story holds true in almost any decade or century.  If you're not familiar with the opera, read the full synopsis of The Rake's Progress.
