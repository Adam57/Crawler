Dead Space 3 Released; February New Releases

Tuesday February 5, 2013

While the month of February contains less noteworthy releases than last month did, the games that are planned for February are all fairly big budget releases starting with Dead Space 3 which was released today. Some PC gamers were upset over being left out of the demo release two weeks ago, but all should be forgotten now that the game is out. Other releases hitting the market include Crysis 3, Aliens: Colonial Marines and a few DLC packs and expansions for games already out.

The Witcher 3 Wild Hunt Announced for 2014, Move Over Skyrim

Tuesday February 5, 2013

CD Projekt RED confirmed what they teased in mid-January, The Witcher 3 is coming. The game has been unveiled in an exclusive interview with Game Informer that's due to hit the web to its subscribers on Wednesdays. A sneak peak at some details were unveiled in a two minute Game Informer coverage trailer released that includes some game play footage and interviews with the development team. Here is what we know so far:

  • The world of The Witcher 3 is huge; 30 times bigger than The Witcher 2 and even larger than The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim world.
  • Open world gaming, allowing players to explore how they want to.
  • No load screens, players adventure seamlessly throughout the world.
  • New game play mechanics called monster hunting.
  • More personal storyline to Gerant and his family.
  • Built for Next Gen Consoles and "high end" PC platforms using the new REDengine 3 game engine.
  • 2014 release date.

More details will surely be unveiled tomorrow when the Game Informer story hits the web so be sure to stay tuned.

PC Gets The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim Dragonborn DLC

Tuesday February 5, 2013

After being available since December 5th on the Xbox 360, the latest The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim DLC has been released for the PC today. The Dragonborn DLC allows players to take their adventuring outside of Skyrim as they travel to the island of Solstheim, as they try to uncover the mystery behind the return of a Dragonborn. In addition to the exploring Solstheim, which was the setting of the Bloodmoon expansion for The Elder Scrolls III Morrowind, the Dragonborn DLC also includes new shouts, spells, powers and most interestingly of all, the ability to mount and fly a dragon.

The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim Dragonborn DLC is the third DLC for the open world role playing game and is available on Steam for $19.99

Call of Duty Black Ops 2 Revolution DLC Out Now

Wednesday January 30, 2013

The Call of Duty Black Ops 2 Revolution DLC has been released today for the Xbox 360 along with a Revolution DLC Gameplay trailer. The Revolution DLC is the first content pack released for Call of Duty Black Ops 2 and is currently exclusive to Xbox 360. However, if it follows previous Call of Duty content packs then it should be out for the PC and PS3 in about a month.

Revolution DLC contains four new mutliplayer maps, a new weapon and a new zombies game mode that lets you play as a zombie. The trailer that has been released is two minutes of pulse pounding action that should provide enough incentive for one to hand over the 1200 Microsoft points it will cost you.
