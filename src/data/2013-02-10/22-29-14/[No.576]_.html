Ravens Parade and Stadium Rally Details

Monday February 4, 2013

The Ravens parade, Baltimore's post-Super Bowl party, will start Tuesday, February 5 at 10:45 a.m. It will step off from City Hall, where a congratulatory banner will hang, and then head south on Commerce Street and continue to Pratt and Howard streets. Ravens players are expected to roll through the streets in more than 30 vehicles, with Baltimore's Marching Ravens--the team band--playing the team fight song and other festive tunes.

Next, a stadium rally will begin at 12:30 p.m. at M&T Bank Stadium, which seats 71,000. Officials expect at least 100,000 fans.

For those of you who are headed downtown, you might also be interested in knowing the National Aquarium will allow Ravens fans to enjoy $10 off general admission tickets on Tuesday. To get the discount, visitors must be wearing Ravens attire or purple clothing.

Currently, the National Weather Service is forecasting temperatures in the low- to mid-30s, with a 20 percent chance of snow.

When and Where is the Super Bowl?

Thursday January 24, 2013

Our own Baltimore Ravens will face off against the San Francisco 49ers in the NFL championship this year, leaving many to wonder when and where the Super Bowl takes place--and where to tune in to watch. Below are the answers to all your burning questions, as well as some additional links on how to gear up for the big game.

When is the Super Bowl?
Super Bowl XLVII is set to kick off at 6:30 E.S.T. on Sunday, February 3.

Where is the Super Bowl?
The game will take place in New Orleans at the Mercedes-Benz Superdome.

What TV Station is the Super Bowl on?
CBS will be airing the game this year, which means Baltimoreans can tune in to WJZ-13. According to the Baltimore Ravens official website, the TV announcers will include Jim Nantz (play-by-play), Phil Simms (analyst), Solomon Wilcots (sideline), and Steve Tasker (sideline).

What radio station is the Super Bowl on?
In Baltimore, 98 Rock F.M. and WBAL-1090 A.M. will air the game. Those with Sirius satellite radios can tune to channel 88 or 92 to listen in. Locally, the radio announcers will include Gerry Sandusky (play-by-play), Stan White (analyst), and Qadry Ismail (analyst).

What color jerseys will the Ravens wear?
The Ravens will be wearing white jerseys during the Super Bowl game.

How can I send off the Ravens?
Baltimore will host a Super Bowl Sendoff Rally on Monday, January 28 at the Inner Harbor Amphitheater(corner of Pratt and Light streets). The Ravens cheerleaders and team mascot, Poe, will entertain the masses beginning at 10:30 a.m., with the officially rally beginning at 11:30 a.m. Current players and coaches, as well as members of the 2000 Super Bowl XXXV team, are scheduled to arrive at the rally at 12:15 p.m.
