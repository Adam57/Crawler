Delicious Dumplings

Thursday February 7, 2013
Dumplings are not a food I grew up eating. My mother does not like any bread or bread-like item that doesn't have a substantial crust, which automatically eliminates dumplings! I started loving them as an adult. There's nothing more comforting on a snowy evening than a rich stew or casserole topped with some tender and fluffy dumplings. Enjoy.

Last Minute Pasta Recipes

Try Clam Linguine, Chicken and Fettuccine, or Spaghetti Carbonara for starters. With a green salad and something simple for dessert you're done cooking for the day.
