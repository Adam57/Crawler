Soy May Reduce Lung Cancer Risk in Women

Friday February 8, 2013

This week over on lungcancer.about.com, About Guide Lynne Eldridge, MD, posted some recent findings on soy--namely that soy intake may reduce lung cancer risk in women. Because those of you who have followed my blog for a long time know that I believe that, based on research, replacing dairy with soy and plant-based proteins is a good step toward good health, I thought I'd share! And, Lynne linked to to my article on Ways to Cook with Tofu. :)

Thanks for the informative article, Lynne!

Dairy-Free Gluten-Free Chocolate Peanut Butter Brownies

Wednesday January 30, 2013

I'm not sure what it is about the Super Bowl that makes me crave chocolate and peanut butter--whether it's just that everyone likes chocolate and peanut butter or that the combination is simple and fun--but whatever it is, I like it. Every year among the spread of dips, chips, and finger foods, I always manage to make at least one dessert that showcases the duo, whether it's vegan buckeye bars, chocolate cupcakes with peanut butter frosting, or some type of peanut butter and chocolate cookie. These Dairy-Free Gluten-Free Peanut Butter Brownies are rich and chocolaty with swirls of peanut-buttery goodness, and they will definitely be at the party this weekend. They are amazing, and no one will ever guess that they were prepared without dairy or gluten ingredients.

If you're like me and you LOVE chocolate and peanut butter treats at your Super Bowl celebration (or any day, really) here are a few more beloved dairy-free favorites:

  • Dairy-Free Peanut Butter Bonbons
  • Easy Chocolate Cupcakes with Peanut Butter Frosting
  • Homemade Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups
  • Peanut Butter Oatmeal Chip Cookies
  • Vegan Buckeye Bars
  • Vegan Peanut Butter Pie
