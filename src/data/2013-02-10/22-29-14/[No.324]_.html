Reserve a Group Study Room
View:

Reserve Room 339 or 340 for group study up to one month ahead of time. You may reserve up to three one-hour time slots per day.

This service is reserved for the NYU community.

Study Room Reservation Rules:
  • Bring your email reservation confirmation form with you to the room.
  • Group study room users without reservations must leave when requested by a group with proof of valid reservation.
  • Rooms may not be reserved for one person only.  If you are studying alone, you may be asked to leave. 
  • If your group (2 or more people) is 15 minutes late from the beginning of your time slot, the reservation is invalid and the room may be given to another group.
  • Rooms cannot be reserved or held by placing personal belongings in them. Unattended items will be removed.
*If the library suspects misuse of the reservation system we have the right to cancel your reservation.
