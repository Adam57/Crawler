Welcome

The EIA seeks to create an entrepreneurial ecosystem based upon NYU Poly's i-squared-e (invention, innovation & entrepreneurship) direction. The club will provide a forum to learn the concepts of entrepreneurship, provide the benefits of self-employment, and the step-by-step path involved in launching and commercializing your own ideas.
