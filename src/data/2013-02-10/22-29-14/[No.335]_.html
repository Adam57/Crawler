Graduate Special / Visiting Student Application & Registration Form

An individual requesting permission to register as a non-degree student is assigned a special student status.  Included in this status are individuals who want to take courses for professional advancement or personal development, or individuals from other universities wishing to transfer credit back to their university, but who do not want to earn a degree.

A visiting student is allowed to take up to nine credit hours maximum and upto only 6 credits(2 courses) per semester at NYU-Poly without formally applying for admission. If a visiting student desires to continue at NYU-Poly as a matriculated student in a graduate degree or certificate program, they must follow the formal application process.

Individuals who wish to register for course/s must fill the below “Special Visiting Student Registration Form.” Upon completion of this form, you will need to submit in a copy of your:

  • Transcripts
  • Resume
  • Photo ID

Please send this documentation by e-mail to vnarayan@poly.edu. A representative from the NYU-Poly Graduate Admissions will contact you with instructions on how to proceed.

Please note that when you register for a course at NYU-Poly, you become responsbile for all tuition and fees associated with your registration. Please click here to review our tuition and fees.
