A Festive Time of Year in the Windy City

Few cities do the holidays better than Chicago. From the annual Christkindlmarket with holiday fare and crafts, to the millions of twinkling lights at the Lincoln Park Zoo and the Brookfield Zoo, the holiday season in Chicago is not to be missed.

Tax-Free Shopping in Chicago This February

Thursday February 7, 2013

If you have any shopping to do this month, might be worth waiting until President's Day weekend (Saturday, February 16 - Monday, February 18) when the Shops at 900 N. Michigan will be offering tax-free shopping with a 9.25% discount at over 40 participating shops, including J.Crew, Michael Kors, MaxMara, Club Monaco, L'Occitane, L.K.Bennett, Solstice Sunglass Boutique, DNA2050, Montblanc, Charles David, Original Penguin, Mezlan, The Shop at Equinox, Galt Toys & Galt Baby, Karen Millen, space519, and more.

Store hours: Monday - Saturday 10 am - 7 pm; Sunday 12 pm - 6 pm.

Parking will also be discounted to the $10 "Alexander Hamilton" parking validation rate with proof of purchase.

All of the details and participating stores can be found on the Tax Free website.

What's Happening This February in Chicago

Thursday January 31, 2013

Chicago Restaurant Week 2013

Thursday January 31, 2013

Chicago Restaurant Week is a citywide "culinary celebration" featuring more than 250 restaurants throughout the Chicago area. The 10-day dining event runs February 1 - 10, 2013 and features prix fixe menus starting at $22 for lunch and $33/$44 for dinner (excluding beverages, tax and gratuity).

Learn more about the event with our Chicago Restaurant Week 2013 Guide. In the guide, you will learn how you can browse a list of participating restaurants, view their Chicago Restaurant Week menus, and make reservations.

Several vegetarian restaurants are also participating, which is good news for those who don't eat meat or diary.
