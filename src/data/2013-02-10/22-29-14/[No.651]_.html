Francesca Battistelli Is Looking Toward a New Album and a New Tour

Friday February 8, 2013

Francesca Battistelli is getting ready for the "Hundred More Years Tour" and the March 12th release of Hundred More Years Deluxe Edition. The tour, which will kick off right before the album release (on March 7), will feature The Afters, Kyle Sherman and Love & The Outcome. The four popular artists will visit nearly 20 cities in Canada and the U.S. through early May.

RED - 'Release The Panic'

Thursday February 7, 2013

RED rocks it, rocks it harder and then shows a fun side (if you listen to the Deluxe Version) with remixes that come out better than you would think! The ballads included on Release The Panic seemed to throw some fans (like my daughter, who wailed, "Where's the metal?") but their beauty soon overcame her panic.

Free Music From Skrip

Thursday February 7, 2013

If you're looking for music to help get you through your Thursday, Skrip, a Chicago native who has been rapping for Jesus since the young age of 12, can hook you up. He is offering his album, The Und_rscore II as a free download. The follow-up to The Und_rscore and his debut album Becoming All Things, The Und_rscore II tackles issues frequently glamorized by mainstream rappers like sex, drugs, money, and pipe dreams - not to make them sound great, but to show how damaging they are to our youth and our culture.

Newsboys Announce 2013 Tour

Tuesday February 5, 2013

The Inpop band newsboys will be back on the road this month for the third leg of the "God's Not Dead" Tour.  Sharing fantastic music from God's Not Dead, their last release, they will be again be joined by Building 429 for the half of the tour and the second half will feature Britt Nicole, All Things New and CAMPBELL.
