Airline Offers Feng Shui Seating

Saturday February 9, 2013

Those looking for a little luck on their next flight might want to consider where they sit. JetStar, an Asian airline, has unveiled its feng shui seating chart, according to CMM.

Passengers booking seat enter their date of birth, gender, and what they are looking for this year: health, wealth, career, love, or friendship and then are given seat assignments based on this information. For example, those looking for wealth should sit in row six and those wanting to make new friends should sit in rows 3, 4, 13, 14, 23 or 24.

Man Sells Cans of Clean Air in Beijing

Sunday February 3, 2013

In response to Beijing's recent bout of smoggy days, a Chinese billionaire has taken to the streets to sell cans of clean air, according to this video report.

The yellow cans of 'clean air' are filled with the air from provinces far from Beijing and were sold on the streets of Beijing this week for 5RMB, the proceeds of which are going to charity. The stunt was the man's way of bringing attention to China's ever-growing air pollution problems. Cheers to that!

Li Na To Play in Australian Open Final

Wednesday January 23, 2013

China's Li Na has advanced to the Australian Open final after defeating world number two Maria Sharapova today.

Li won in straight-sets 6-2, 6-2 during the semi-finals. Li, who is ranked sixth in the world, will play in the women's singles final on Saturday against the winner of today's second semi-final match between Victoria Azarenka of Belarus and Sloane Stephens of the U.S.
