Favorite Apps: Roadside America

Thursday February 7, 2013

I have an unexplainable attraction for tourism kitsch. Tell me about a giant orange that doubles as a refreshment stand, a water tower that's painted like a teapot or a giant fire helmet beside the road and I'm there. I've long been a fan of Roadside America as the source of offbeat tourist attraction info, and just in case your affliction is as bad as mine, it's easy to get a fix any time by using their app.

POLL: Smart Phone Apps for Travel

Wednesday February 6, 2013

These days, lots of attractions, museums, places and sights have their own app. Just download our app, their websites say and somehow they imply that you'll have more fun or enjoy the place more. We've tried quite a few and frankly, our results are mixed - and apps come and go. Because of that, we've been hesitant to include them in our California travel information.

Last month, we asked whether you use apps when you travel and the results are clear: about half of all folks who responded use them regularly and three-fourths us them at least occasionally. Look for more mentions of them, app reviews and more in the months to come.
