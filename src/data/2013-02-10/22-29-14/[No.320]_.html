New at the library: The best science books of 2012

Like last year, we have scanned all the best-books-of-the-year lists to bring you the most acclaimed science writing of the 2012. So whether you resolved to read more this year or simply want a break from text books, drop by the library to check these books out. They are displayed in the new books section on the right hand side of the lower level.
