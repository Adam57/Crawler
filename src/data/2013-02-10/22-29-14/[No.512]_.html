Hot Chocolate for a blizzard. . .

Friday February 8, 2013

If you live in the Northeast, then you're most likely experiencing winter storm Nemo. I live smack in the center of the warning area, and the snow is piling up and the winds are howling. There's nothing like a few feet of snow to make you crave something warm and soothing, and nothing answers that craving better than a steaming mug of hot chocolate. I'm not talking about the kind you get from a package, but the real deal--made with fresh milk, good-quality chocolate, sugar and vanilla.

Enjoy, and for those of you in my neck of the woods--happy shoveling!
